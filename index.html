Our Story

The Journey of Srimadhur Amrutha Chaha

2012

The Beginning: Sai Enterprises

Sai Enterprises was started in 2012 by Mr. Vikas M. Vajare with the aim of making easy-to-use, hygienic, and tasty tea and coffee premixes. The focus was on maintaining consistent taste and quality for tea stalls, offices, and food businesses.

From the beginning, the company worked on using good raw materials, standard processes, and perfecting recipes to meet customer needs.

2019

Birth of Srimadhur Amrutha Chaha

With growing experience and confidence, Srimadhur Amrutha Chaha was launched in 2019 as a dedicated consumer-facing brand under the manufacturing strength of Sai Enterprises.

The brand was created to celebrate the emotional connection Indians have with tea - a beverage that brings people together, energizes mornings, and fuels conversations throughout the day. The name Srimadhur Amrutha Chaha reflects our belief that tea is the nectar (Amrutha) of daily life, bringing sweetness and warmth to every moment.
